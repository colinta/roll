# Example

###### Scenario

Player needs to first roll a 4 or higher using a `d10` or a `d6` wild dice, _and
then_ needs to roll a 6 or higher using a `d12` with a `+1` modifier, or a `d6`
wild dice.

```
$ d10,d6/4 1+d12,d6/6

|  STATS  |
+---------+
 d10
-----
min: 1
max: 10
avg: 5.5

 d6
----
min: 1
max: 6
avg: 3.5

 1 + d12
---------
min: 2
max: 13
avg: 7.5

+---------------+
|  PERCENTAGES  |

d10,d6
------
1: 15 of 60 (25.0%)

6: 15 of 60 (25.0%)
7: 6 of 60 (10.0%)
8: 6 of 60 (10.0%)

10: 6 of 60 (10.0%)

1+d12,d6
--------
```
